# Content Generator

## What it does

Gives you an approval-ready content pipeline grounded in what your audience is actually discussing right now.

Researches current conversations, then turns the signals into:
- topic briefs and priority angles
- an editorial calendar and strategic pillars
- hooks, outlines, and draft copy in each channel's native format
- approval notes

Every market claim is tied to a cited source or your provided context, and thin or single-source evidence is flagged rather than smoothed over. It checks saved history first to avoid repeating angles you have already used or rejected.

## Setup

Set your audience, positioning, channels, approval rules, and claim standards in `agent/instructions.md`.
