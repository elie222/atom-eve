# Support Replies

## What it does

Turns your open Intercom conversations into grounded draft replies your team can review and send. It reads open conversations, including the retrieved conversation parts, and prepares:

- draft replies grounded in the thread and your product context
- unclear cases that need more information
- escalations for sensitive or high-risk issues (billing, refunds, security, legal, angry customers)

Every draft stays paired with its conversation id and customer context.

## Setup

Provide an Intercom access token with read access to conversations. Add your product documentation, refund and billing rules, escalation paths, and support tone to `agent/instructions.md`.
